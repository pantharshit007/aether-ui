## Usage

You can customize the content of the morphing card by passing the following props to the component.

- `title`: The title of the morphing card.
- `description`: The description of the morphing card.
- `cancelButtonText`: The text of the cancel button.
- `confirmButtonText`: The text of the confirm button.
- `dialogTitle`: The title of the dialog.
- `dialogDescription`: The description of the dialog.
- `dialogCancelText`: The text of the cancel button in the dialog.
- `dialogConfirmText`: The text of the confirm button in the dialog.
- `onConfirm`: The callback function to be called when the confirm button is clicked.
